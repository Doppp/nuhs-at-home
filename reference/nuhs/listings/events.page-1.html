    NUHS@Home 5th Anniversary Grand Round

    Since the first patient was admitted to NUHS@Home in September 2020, hospital-at-home has emerged as a sustainable model of care delivery, advancing home-first healthcare in Singapore, and enabling patients to receive hospital-level treatment in their own homes.

    The celebrations included talks by A/Prof Dan Yock Young, Mr Lim Cher Wee, and Dr Norshima Nashi. The event closed with a fireside chat moderated by Dr Jason Phua.

    We were graced by our Guest-of-Honour, Professor Kenneth Mak, as we looked ahead to NUHS@Home's 2030 vision and scaling up to 400 beds and beyond. With over 300 participants, 48 posters, and 12 booths - 7 of which were by external partners.

  • 08 Nov 2024

    Hospital at Home Scientific Symposium 2024

    NUHS@Home is proud to have hosted the first-ever Hospital at Home Scientific Symposium in Singapore! With the theme Building a Community of Practice, Research, and Innovation, we brought together voices from clinical, academic, and policy fields across Singapore and even some friends from Malaysia to share ideas and discuss future directions.

    We were honored to have Dr Kok Mun Fong as our Guest of Honour, with inspiring keynote talks by Prof Chien Earn Lee and Prof Jason Phua, ending with a panel expertly moderated by Prof Nick Sevdalis, PhD. With over 200 participants, 30 posters and 4 oral presentations across the topics of Clinical, Implementation, Education and QI.

  • 12 Oct 2024

    Health Together Carnival 2024 at Jurong

    NUHS@Home debut our first community engagement at the NUHS Health Together Carnival held at Jurong Spring Community Centre. The NUHS@Home team on the ground took the opportunity to create more awareness about mobile inpatient care, the benefits of home recovery as well as the care services that NUHS@Home provides to our patients on their recovery journey.

    The health and wellness event attracted more than 1,500 residents and those we spoke with were curious and receptive to this relatively new model of care in Singapore.
