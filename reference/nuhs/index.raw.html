NUHS@Home
Hospital-Level Care in the Comfort of Your Home

What is NUHS@Home?

The NUHS@Home, also known as Mobile Inpatient Care or MIC@Home in Singapore, is an innovative hospital-at-home programme designed for eligible patients who prefer to receive hospital-level medical care in a familiar environment.

Our patients make their recovery at home and are looked after by a dedicated multi-disciplinary team of doctors, nurses and allied health professionals who will monitor their condition remotely via smart devices as well as home visits where required. 

Types of NUHS@Home services:

Our NUHS@Home programme provides a wide range of medical services tailored to each patient’s needs: 

Remote vital signs monitoring
Intravenous (IV) infusions or injections
Laboratory investigations such as blood or urine tests
Home visits by doctors, nurses and/or therapists, supported by virtual consultations and a 24/7 dedicated telephone number.
Patients can be assured that the services and treatment they receive at home are similar to the care they receive in the hospital
