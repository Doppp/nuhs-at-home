What is NUHS@Home?

NUHS@Home, also known as Mobile Inpatient Care, is a hospital-at-home programme designed for eligible patients who prefer to receive hospital-level care in a familiar environment.

Patients recover at home while doctors, nurses and allied health professionals monitor their progress remotely and through home visits where required.

Types of NUHS@Home services

The programme provides a range of hospital-level services tailored to each patient's needs.

Remote vital signs monitoring

Patients are monitored remotely using smart devices and structured daily review.

Intravenous infusions and injections

Treatments that usually require a ward stay can be delivered safely at home.

Laboratory investigations

Blood and urine tests remain part of the care pathway without repeated hospital transfers.

Home visits and virtual consultations

Doctors, nurses and therapists combine in-person care with video review.

Hospital-equivalent treatment

Patients receive treatment aligned with inpatient pathways while staying at home.

How does NUHS@Home work?

The official programme flow centres on referral, suitability assessment, home setup and daily review.

01

Referral and assessment

Patients are referred by their doctor and assessed for safety and clinical suitability.

02

Enrollment and setup

The care team arranges equipment, instructions and the treatment plan for the home setting.

03

Daily review

Doctors conduct daily video consultations, supported by home visits where necessary.

04

Nursing procedures

Nurses perform procedures such as IV drips, injections and blood tests at home.

05

Shared patient role

Patients remain contactable, stay at home and monitor vital signs as instructed.

Why choose NUHS@Home?

The official programme highlights better recovery in familiar surroundings, greater family presence, treatment delivered to the doorstep and inpatient-style subsidy support.

Better recovery in familiar surroundings

Home can reduce stress and lower the risk of hospital-acquired infection.

Home is where the heart is

No restrictive visiting hours, and recovery happens around loved ones and daily routines.

Same treatment without the inconvenience

The medical team brings care and treatment directly to the patient's doorstep.

Same subsidy framework as inpatient care

Patients continue to benefit from the same subsidy and insurance structures used in hospital care.
